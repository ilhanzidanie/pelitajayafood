Our Products

Cassava Chips

With the frying process using quality oil and supervised by experienced officers, and using some additional secret spices that have been passed down from generation to generation and without using harmful chemicals, this cassava chip has a strong, delicious and crunchy taste when eaten.

Potato Chips

snacks from real potatoes, crunchy and full of flavour, hygienically and modernly processed without preservatives and MSG. Available in wavy and flat shapes.

Spinach Chips

chips made from spinach leaves and fried using flour that has been seasoned. Usually the taste is salty with a savory onion aroma. Spinach chips are one of the new snacks that people are interested in because the raw materials for making them can be developed in highland areas or lowland areas.
